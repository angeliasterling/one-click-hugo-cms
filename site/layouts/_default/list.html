Self-Publishing

I'm going to start this out by saying I don't know much about the publishing industry in general and I'm certainly no expert (I'm very much learning as I go). I can't say if self-publishing is better or worse than traditional publishing, but it seems to really come down to personal preference. If you write a quality book there's a good chance readers will find you either way.
